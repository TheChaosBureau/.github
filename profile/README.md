# The Chaos Bureau  

_Intellectual disaster, with letterhead._

## About 
  
**The Chaos Bureau** is a fake company, which is unfortunate because that may be the most honest thing about it.

We are an AI-generated, AI-slop research bureaucracy, full of imaginary employees, fake meetings, damaged corporate badges & egos, procedural nonsense, profanity, technical arguments, and a dog who may or may not legally be chairman.
  
Underneath the costume, it is one very real engineer's attempt to put very serious, but unfinished, and absolutely uncredentialed work into the world without pretending they have permission.

The Bureau is basically an [*Inside Out*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inside_Out)-style control room for the research process: skepticism, ambition, failure, physical intuition, pattern-hunting, rigor, and bullshit all yelling across the conference table.

The characters are fake, the company is fake, the confidence is provisional, and the whole thing is probably ridiculous — but the questions are real, the graphs are usually real, and the point is to find out what survives contact with measurement.

Fake meetings. Real graphs. Imaginary peer review. Please sign the waiver.
